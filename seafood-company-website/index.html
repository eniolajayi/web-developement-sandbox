About company

Established in 1991 by jonathan Morisson as a small service oriented seafood provider for local reestaurants and markets. PSDNION Seafood Co. has grown into a multi-million dollar seafood company providing the best in fresh seafood for all Southern California.

Despite the continued growth of the family owned and operated company. two standards remain a constant. Quality and service continue to be the highest priority for the company. Live and fresh seafood are brought in on a daily basis and deivered to our customers promptly in our fleet of refrigerated trucks

The goal of our company is to provide our customers with the best seafood products available at competitive prices and an excelllent standard of service so that we can continue to grow with the prosperity of our clients. Poseidonions Seafood Co. is an HACCP certified seafood company, in which our seafood is handled through consistent certified inspections to ensure the freshest quality seafood for our customers. Unified is the first choice for whosesalers, distributors and major supermarkets.

How we do it

We use our commercial fishing vessel. the Moonlight and the vessels of our partners to bring the oceans bounty directly to you. We catch and source wild caught seafood and deliver it to local farmers marker direct from the boat.

Everything is delivered fresh and natural-nothing is flash frozen, farm raised, or with color addded form wild salmon to sea scallops, albacore tuna, California halibut, butterfish, petrale sole and more
